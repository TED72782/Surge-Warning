What is this?
A logistic regression model predicting whether mPEDOCS will cross 100 — the ED tipping point — within the next 12 or 24 hours. Trained on 8,748 hours of 2025 data from a pediatric ED.

mPEDOCS ≥ 100 is where median Arrival-to-Room time jumps from 22 → 32 min and the 90th percentile crosses 2 hours. It marks the transition from a manageable queue to a decompensating one.

24-Hour Model
AUC 0.83 · At p ≥ 0.15: sensitivity 72%, specificity 81%, ~2,250 alerts/yr. Outperforms 12h due to the 24h circadian periodicity of mPEDOCS — today's state predicts tomorrow's same-hour state better than what happens at the midpoint.

Alert Tiers
LOW <10% · Normal operations
WATCH 10–20% · Charge RN awareness
ADVISORY 20–35% · Alert bed management. Proactive discharge rounds.
WARNING 35–50% · House supervisor. Pre-position float staff. Overflow ready.
IMMINENT ≥50% · Initiate surge protocol. Same as mPEDOCS=100 response — early.
