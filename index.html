What is this?
A logistic regression model predicting whether mPEDOCS will cross 100 within the next 12 or 24 hours. Trained on 8,748 hours of 2025 data. mPEDOCS ≥ 100 is the tipping point where median Arrival-to-Room time jumps from 22 → 83 min and the 90th percentile crosses 2 hours.
